About.

Welcome to Whally Clicker! This game was developed for the blue mob, by the blue mob team!

How to play.

Click the mouse! each click earns points. points can be spent on upgrades to increase point production.
Passive points can be earned by purchasing the passive upgrades. passives only yield points while the AFK timer is active. each click grants the user 3 seconds of afk time, up to a maximum of 900 seconds (15 minutes).
Players may choose to redeem their excess points for nfts, tokens, seasonal rewards, or other in-game perks. these items may change between seasons. points spent do not reduce your total point score for the seasonal leaderboard.
